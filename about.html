About GEPAM Science Hub

GEPAM Science Hub is an educational platform dedicated to making Physics and Chemistry learning simple, accessible and effective for students from Form 1 to Form 6.

We provide simplified notes, past papers, solutions and learning resources designed to help students understand science concepts and improve their academic performance.

🎯 Our Mission

To simplify science education by providing quality, affordable and accessible learning materials that help students build confidence and achieve better results.

🚀 Our Vision

To become a leading science learning platform in Tanzania by making Physics and Chemistry easy for every student.

📚 What We Offer

📖 Science Notes

Simple Physics and Chemistry notes from Form 1 to Form 6.

📝 Past Papers

Examinations from Annual, Mock, Joint, Pre-NECTA and NECTA with preparation support.

🎯 Examination Preparation

Resources designed to improve understanding, confidence and examination performance.
